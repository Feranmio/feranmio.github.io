Java Servlets

I had made a version of a petition website in 2017 and felt the need to update it. Halfway through the revision I felt it was bare and a crowd funding feature seemed like the right fit. In the course of this project I had multiple ideas on things to create in the near future. Going over my old code during this project revision, it made me realise the growth I had attained thus far as a developer.

Dashboard

View data on created petitions and fundraisers including analytics to show user interaction.

Crowd Funding

Create new fundraisers and petitions to support a positive cause.

Comments

Join the conversation and share your views on the petitions and fundraisers.

Update

Update petitions and fundraisers to keep concerned parties up-to-date with the status of the cause.

Settings

Update profile, change password, and request for funds from fundraiser.

Share

Share links of fundraisers and petitions on different social media platforms.
