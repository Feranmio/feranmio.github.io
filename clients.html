Pristine Digital, 2019

Static Website

A month after the "Showcase" went live, my brother linked me with a new digital marketing company that needed some work done. I was excited to be a part of the project and wanted to ensure they were happy with the end result so I took time to understand the ethos of the company and their expectations. After a couple weeks of collaboration we produced a digital aesthetic fitting for the company.

Homepage

In-line with the clients vision, the homepage has a sophisticated look that easily displays the company colour and their Unique Value Preposition at first glance.

Responsive

The amazing user experience of the website remains consistent across different devices, screen sizes and orientations.

Moving Text

The moving text effect gives the static website a heartbeat of some sort, especially when it is left idle.

Custom Icons

Representing the creative process of the company with customised icons that fits perfectly with the theme of the website.

Layout

Items are displayed in an organised and responsive layout that highlights the content.

Contact Form

Users can send direct messages to the admin webmail of the website.
